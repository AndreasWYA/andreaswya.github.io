MathAdvance

MathAdvance is a Mobile Gamified Learning App for Kids to better engage them in learning and studying Mathematics. The app features Multiple Mini Games for kids to play while also helping them in learning and engaging in Mathematics with multiple subjects and topics available. After that they can do daily missions inside the app to help them gain points and even unlock achievements. Then they can connect with their friends by adding them inside the game and they can even compare each other's badges and achievements from playing the app.
